symplistic.ai Announces Launch of AI Agent Platform to Drive Enterprise Efficiency.



Today, symplistic.ai, a next-generation AI company, officially announced its mission to build enterprise-grade AI agents designed to streamline operations, personalize experiences, and deliver real-time insights across regulated industries.

Built on the belief that AI should go beyond static automation, symplistic.ai is developing task-oriented, intelligent agents that can observe, reason, and act within enterprise workflows and not just respond to prompts. These agents are purpose-built to reduce operational overhead, improve decision-making, and free up human teams to focus on higher-value work.

“We’re entering a new era of intelligent automation,” said Steven Astorino, Chief Executive Officer of symplistic.ai. “Our AI agents are designed to plug directly into enterprise systems, understand business context, and execute real tasks — from financial insights to customer support — all while remaining explainable, secure, and compliant.”

symplistic.ai’s initial focus is on the banking and financial services sector, where efficiency, trust, and personalization are critical. The company’s agents are being designed to handle a wide range of functions, including onboarding, document analysis, fraud detection, and financial health insights — with expansion into other industries already underway.

The company’s AI agents are built to be easily integrated into enterprise environments, offering organizations access to intelligent, task-oriented solutions designed with explainability, security, and compliance at the core.

As organizations look to accelerate their AI strategies, symplistic.ai aims to bridge the gap between large language models and enterprise-grade impact, turning generative intelligence into measurable outcomes.

symplistic.ai builds domain-specific AI agents that integrate seamlessly into enterprise workflows. Focused on real-world tasks, compliance, and explainability, symplistic.ai helps organizations unlock the full potential of intelligent automation safely and at scale.
